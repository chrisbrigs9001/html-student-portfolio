About Me

I am Christopher and I am currently a Senior Software Analyst for Digital Dream Forge LLC located in Tennessee. I have been working in the QA field for almost 3 years now and have rapidly learned many skills to help in my journey. Before my time as QA, I worked in Facilities Maintenance in the entertainment sector in Country Clubs and Bowling Alleys. There, I learned practical and hands on problem solving skills to fix and maintain building infrastructure, plumbing, HVAC, and even Pinsetter/Pinspotter machines. I managed the workflow of several employees, and was also in control of maintaining inventory for basic facility and janitorial needs as well as parts for any routine and emergency mechanical repairs needed.
